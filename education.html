What We Do

National Mentoring Summit:

Yearly gathering of mentors, mentees, parents, professionals, civic society leaders, educators and government representatives across the country to share ideas on how to close the mentoring gap in the country and the development of our communities; to contemplate quality mentoring, quality education, research and policies and, enterprising youth leadership.

Leaders and Mentors Summit/Camp:

Annual interactive leadership and mentoring camp meeting where Mentors spend quality time with their Mentees. Pacesetters in specific spheres of influence are interviewed to share their unique experiences, challenges and successes in navigating their fields of influence.

The DreamsAfrica Leadership and Mentoring Academy:

DreamsAfrica academy with well-designed curriculum to equip selected young potential leaders across Africa to be commissioned for transformational assignments in their institutions, countries and communities. This is a residential scholarship program with highly competitive criteria for selection including demonstrable leadership potential with disruptive ideas to advance the human race. Selected leaders are tasked to lead projects, initiatives and to design strategies for successful implementation of new ideas. They are also assigned to successful industry experts, CEOs and, multi-national institutions for early leadership development support, career development, networking and funding.

Beyond Emotions & Reason Summit (One-week Family Retreat):

Half year family retreats and tours aimed at helping leaders, mentors and families to intentionally reconnect with their loved ones. This is to promote the culture and values of love and responsible parenting in refreshingly new ways as families share their success stories as well as challenges with like-minded community of friends. To provide social support system and counselling for struggling relationships.

The Leadership Challenge/Campaign/Rally (National Mentoring Day):

A day dedicated to celebrating mentoring and mentors. We create awareness on the need for mentoring.

Camp David (Leadership Retreat):

Leadership retreat for DreamsAfrica Leaders, Volunteers and the Pioneers of Mentoring organizations who have been mentored by DreamsAfrica.

High Potential Leaders (HiPOLs) Camp:

Mentoring Package for Junior High school students for self-discovery, purpose discovery and leadership.

Careers, Vocations and Callings (CVC):

Prisons mission, Outreach to Rehabilitation centers, Orphanages and villages. Self-worth, hope and talent development campaigns among the vulnerable.
