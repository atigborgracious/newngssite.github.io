About Founder

AJ has over 26 years of experience working with people from different walks of life and has travelled widely across continents. In these years he has mastered the art of communication and the necessary ingredients needed to successfully and effectively pass across a message or idea. Although he is astute in the field of operations, his passion and heart lies in mentoring, coaching and counseling people.
AJ has a passion to help people achieve their goals and develop their interests towards a certain career path. He has hands on experience in people management and his track record has shown that he is a team player as well as Leader.
AJ has a B.COM as well as an MBA degree, He also has several leadership and I.T certifications. Worthy to note is the SMG Coach Accreditation –Training conducted by Strategic Management Group (SMG, Singapore).
AJ is the co-founder of CRE and has been the MD & CEO of the company since 2014. Prior to founding CRE, he was the COO of GLS Publishing in India. Before GLS, AJ worked with Essel Propack, a global tube-packaging company headquartered in Mumbai, India. Essel Propack is a part of Essel Group which specializes in the packaging and manufacturing of laminated plastic tubes for the FMCG and Pharma space.
In a career spanning over two decades, AJ has worked in the Trading and Manufacturing sector, where he performed various functions such as Commercial, Finance, HR, Production, Publishing, Planning, Purchase, Logistics, Distribution and Marketing.
AJ also serves as Country Director - India & Africa for SW Press Co. Ltd, which is one of the world’s largest printing presses located in South Korea.
