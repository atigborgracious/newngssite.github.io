ABOUT ARIYANA

Ariyana Tower is an ‘A’ grade, luxury residential tower located in Dubai’s world-famous development called Dubai Marina. Dubai Marina, a real estate development with an artificial canal stretching 3 km along the Persian Gulf Shoreline was developed by Dubai’s well known and famous property developer Emaar Properties. It was constructed by Dubai’s well known and prestigious contractor that made sure the owners (Lailak Investment LLC) expectation to deliver an exceptional quality building was met with high standard construction and quality finishes, a testimony evident when one is inside the building.

Ariyana Tower is a high-rise tower located just at the beginning of the Marina Walk and adjacent to Sheikh Zayed Road. It consists of three levels of basement floor, a ground floor with retail outlets and 31 residential floors. The property has well-appointed Studio apartments on the first floor, very spacious marina view three-bedroom apartments with a maid’s room and very spacious two-bedroom apartments on the floors between the 02nd and 29th floors. Every apartment is provided with a refrigerator, electric cooking range, oven, microwave, washing machine, dryer and wardrobes. For its residents leisure and recreational activities the tower has a world class gym from Technogym, an outdoor spacious swimming pool for adults and children, separate steam room for men and women, a children’s playroom and a mini soccer field. The Tower has 24 hours manned security with CCTV surveillance, in-house 24 hours maintenance team and a very friendly concierge staff to meet the requirements of its residents.

Ariyana Tower has easy and convenient accessibility with plenty mode of transport easily available outside such as the Metro (Red line), Tram (Al Sufouh) service, Bus service, Water bus service and taxis. Malls, Hotels, Bars, restaurants, Office blocks, Clinics, pharmacies, supermarkets, beauty parlors etc are all within walking distance of the tower. Dubai Marina is also next to Dubai Internet City, Dubai Media City and Dubai Knowledge Park. The world-famous development, a man-made island, Palm Jumeirah is adjacent to Dubai Marina and can be accessed by Al Sufouh tram service. Some its nearby attractions and recreational activities include Dubai International Marina City, Sky Dive Dubai, Zip line, Aqua Venture Water park, Dolphin Bay, Jumeirah Beach Walk and Emirates Golf Club.
