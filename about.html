We are a clothing and accessories brand. We make Lace products customized to our customer’s requirements. Lace is an Irish art and Lace made clothes & accessories were worn by people mainly in, Venice, Rome, Antwerp, Cologne, and Paris, and were dedicated, in high-flown phrases, to the "profit and contentment of beautiful and skilful ladies”. Later, this art spread to other parts of the world including India. This Irish art in India is practiced by women especially in West Godavari District of Andhra Pradesh. This is one of the finest Handmade Crafts in the world and we bring it to you at your doorstep.
