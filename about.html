About Us

Who We Are

Cleft Rock Enterprises Pvt. Ltd (CRE) was incorporated in 2013 as a Private Limited Company with a unique vision to create profitable business that encourage and facilitate other enterprising youth to set up other business.

CRE is one of the growing Print & Publishing Solutions Company in India. We cover every aspect of a Publisher’s Printing Requirements with specialization and expertise in printing Religious, School Books and Educational Print Materials. At CRE we are committed to adopt new technology to ensure that we can always offer our customers the benefits of the latest production methods.

CRE’s clients spread mainly in US, Europe, Africa, and Middle East other than India.

CRE was founded by business practitioners who have combined experience of over 100 years in business and social service. CRE’s businesses are driven by passion and it exists to set up other businesses and hence ensures continual motivation and commitment to operational goals.

Our Team

A J Joseph
MD & CEO

AJ is the co-founder of CRE and serves as the MD & CEO of the company since 01 June 2014. He is an Operations professional with over 25 years of experience in management, commercial and operational aspects of business. Prior to founding CRE, he was the COO of GLS Publishing in India. Before GLS, AJ worked with Essel Propack , a global tube-packaging company headquartered in Mumbai in India. EP is the part of Essel Group, and is a specialty packaging manufacturer of laminated plastic tubes for the FMCG and Pharma space. In a career spanning across two decades, AJ has worked in Trading, Manufacturing sectors across various functions such as Commercial, Finance, HR, Production, Publishing, Planning, Purchase, Logistics, Distribution and Marketing. AJ has extensive experience in packaging, publishing and printing industry and has excellent skills in developing new business and networks. AJ also serves as Country Director-India & Africa for SW Press Co. Ltd, a world's largest printing press in South Korea. AJ is a commerce graduate and holds an Executive MBA with a specialization in Operations.
