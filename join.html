How To Join

Why The DreamsAfrica Mentoring Program?

The DreamsAfrica Mentoring assures the young ones - our Mentees in the Senior High Schools and the Tertiary institutions that they matter and the elders in our Communities care about them. The Program guarantees listening ears, lifting hands and caring hearts to our mentees as they are guided by responsible adults in handling daily challenges. Research confirms that quality mentoring relationships have transformational impacts on the young ones -- unleashing their potential, building their confidence, setting academic goals, encouraging them in their career pursuits and professional development.
Many young ones in single-parent household, face opportunity gap – social, economic and psychological support.

Are you a parent, guardian, a career coach, teacher, or a responsible adult who will want to volunteer as a mentor?

A guide to decision making to volunteer as a mentor. Ask yourself the following questions:
-Am I willing to commit my time to be a listening ear, caring heart, a lifting hand to a lonely youth?
-What age range of youth am I passionate about and interested in working with?
-Would I prefer individual (One-on-one mentoring) or group mentoring?
-Would I prefer being a co-mentor with a colleague mentor?
-How will I want to support a mentee? Skills development, career development or leadership development?
-Which region will I want to be a volunteer Mentor?
-Which institution or community would I want to provide mentorship?

BENEFIT TO MENTOR

-Helps them to better connect with their own children
-There is a sense of fulfilment in helping transform the life of a youth
-Helps them to better understand the youth culture
-A way of reconnecting with self and their sense of purpose

Find a Mentor

Are you a determined and ambitious young person who wants to connect with a mentor? Mentors are supportive individuals who build regulated mentoring relationships with you by offering you guidance, support, and encouragement to help you actualize your potential. These Mentors do not replace your parents and guardians. They help you in making discerning choices, developing your confidence and self-esteem and they provide wise counsel.

Become an Ambassador

Stories connect us to each other, they motivate us to take action and they inspire us to give back. We all have stories to share and our collective voice will help propel the mentoring movement forward. It all begins with sharing your story. Do you have a mentor you want to thank? Someone who made or is making a tremendous difference in your life? Or maybe you are a mentor and have had a transformational experience mentoring a young person? We want to hear from you. There is no better way to understand the impact mentoring can have then hearing directly from who benefit. Though each mentoring story is different, they are all inspiring. Stories show how the mentoring effect has a profound impact not only on mentees, but also mentors.
