Publishing Services

Content Re-purposing

Every publisher’s key asset is the content they own. Understanding the demands of a competitive market, we enable publishers to re-use backlist titles, by casting them in different formats, adding value, contemporizing layout styles and giving this content a new lease of life.

Translation Service

With our highly talented translating team we are able to offer quality service in all major Indian languages.

Media Conversion

With the whole world going digital, converting content for the digital medium is an area of focus for us. We have a skilled team that focuses on converting data from existing media to new media… in any format required. From XML, to Websites, to Multi-media CD Roms.

Page Composition

We take on the entire content and pre-press workflow – encompassing all those stages that are needed to convert a manuscript into a print ready document. We're comfortable with all industry standard applications such as InDesign, Quark and Corel and work on the PC and MAC platforms.

Brand Design & Development

Right from creating logos, to translating them into ongoing brand identity extensions, we have created brand identities for companies and products – and further extended these to establish the identity across different media. Acting virtually as the Brand Identity watchdogs, we monitor the usage of brands across different media.
