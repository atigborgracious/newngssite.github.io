PROGRAMS

REVIVE ASIA

Our Vision is Revival and Kingdom Expansion for the Continent of Asia. We are uniting together with one heart with Ministers and Prayer Partners from across the Globe to raise one voice before God to establish and expand the Kingdom of God in Asia. Through preaching the gospel, Healing and Bringing Restoration to the hearts of people.

THE HEART CONFERENCE

Missioned at bringing our hearts to that of Christ, expressing his love and compassion towards others, thereby reaching out to the world with a message of love and hope. This is the coming together of legends and generals from different cultures, religion, countries and continents to finally acknowledge the person of God in our realities by a relationship.

HIGHER LIFE SUMMIT.02

OVERFLOW! (WISDOM. POWER. AUTHORITY)
This is visioned at building and igniting relevance in people, educating, reviving, amending and restoring their inner man to his original place in God and function. This program is for initiation of new armies for the end mission.

ART. MUSIC AND AWARD NIGHT (A.MAAN)

This event is identified as a medium to collectively bring the body of Christ together in recognition of the inherent gifts and their ministries, and as united in body, spirit and in one voice, we stretch out our arm of love and grace via music, art, various gifts and talents to world. To heal, amend, restore and revive our society to Gods original intention through worship. We picture a generation set apart to fulfill the mandate of God and his desire over the earth. For these reveals the pre-definition of God’s will on the earth and we represent the armies of this dream.

DIAMONDZ (TALK SHOW WITH WISDOM NWANKWO)

Diamondz is an evangelical platform that is visioned at reflecting the life of God, reaching the lost with the gospel and the message of the kingdom of God. It’s our heart to see nations brought to the knowledge of Jesus.
