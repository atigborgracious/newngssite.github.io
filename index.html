About Us

Quickworks Technical Services LLC, Dubai based company, offer a comprehensive service, that cover entire Interior designing work and execution. Our work team led by proven designers, craftsmen, technical experts, experienced in all sector of interior works ensure a single solution for better Interior concepts.
We specialize in providing turnkey interior solutions for luxury homes and commercial projects. Apart from Turnkey interior solutions, we undertake building maintenance, gypsum works, plumbing& electrical, Wall Cladding, Wooden Paneling, Wall Painting, Wall paper Fixing, Carpentry, A/C Maintenance and Installation, Appliances Installation, Kitchens & wardrobes Installation, Flooring and Tile works etc
As a technical services specialized company we have put our team work and experience to good use and carried out contracts throughout UAE. We always ensuring our clients peace of mind with every project completed on time and within budget.
