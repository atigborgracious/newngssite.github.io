Why Us?

Reliability

Finding a reliable printer to assist in producing your publication can be a challenge. The quality of the finished product is crucial in deciding the success or failure of a publication hence at CRE we understand how important even the minutest of details could be, so we handle each task with acute precision and full responsibility. Years of experience and our renowned customer service has helped us build a reputation for quality that ensures our customers receive the best possible results in the shortest possible time.

Team

From digital to offset, from content to print through fulfillment and every critical step in between, our dedicated team provides smart, strategic solutions for your printing needs. Our skilled staff of designers, account managers, prepress and print specialists come from a range of book manufacturing and publishing backgrounds and ensure that every project is perfectly executed to consistently produce high quality work.

Quality Policy

Quality has been a major contributor to our excellence and success. Our processes are geared to deliver the highest quality products. Periodical audits are conducted to check compliance of the standards. Our aim is to achieve customer satisfaction and meet client requirements through continuous improvements of the Quality Management System, right from Paper, Print Quality, Binding Strength, Logistics and Customer Support.

India Cost Advantage

We operate the youngest and most advanced book presses in India together with binding facilities for soft and hardcover books. Being exporters of paper, we procure huge quantity stocks because of which we have good relations with the Paper mills and paper merchants in India. This helps us in offering very competitive prices to our clients thereby reducing the total project cost to a great extent.
