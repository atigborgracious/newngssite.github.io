VISION

‘‘Mentoring industry savvy leaders, deploying Talents and Ideas for transformational assignments in Africa’’

MISSION

‘‘To model Sustainable Leadership and to establish a Principle-Centered mentoring environment.’’

Scope Of Mission

Our mission is to be the go-to cultivated environment for the discovery of original ideas, viable gifts and talents, protégés’ self-worth and purpose, and, to be the original source of leadership development for the nations. We exist therefore as the source of extraordinary talents, systems and policies that shapes the culture of organizations, educational institutions, communities and nations.

‘‘Sustainable Leadership is Leadership that serves its mission with the given indispensable resources without compromising the leadership and mission of the next generation of leaders.’’ — Kwaku Edem Damanka

THE 7Ps EXPERIENCE
AT DREAMSAFRICA

OUR PHILOSOPHY

The wisdom, understanding and knowledge of truth (Original information) that reintroduces us to our self-concept, self-worth, self-esteem, gifts, purpose and leadership. Our curriculum is designed with the individual uniqueness in mind.

OUR PEOPLE

Like-minded and mission-driven fellows who provoke us to unleash our potential for enterprising and strategic leadership in our chosen spheres of influence. Our Mentors, Sponsors, Mentees, Destiny Agents, Accountability Partners, Parents and Guardians.

OUR PROCESSES

The refers to the sustainable purpose discovery and the leadership development processes that we embrace. These processes help us to develop resilience, character of mind and will, and the needed capacity for leadership and mastery our chosen varied spheres of influence.

OUR PRINCIPLES

Enduring principles that guarantee leadership success and, fulfillment in the pursuit of our individual and collective purpose. Principles that mold our belief system, attitudes, character and mindset, and dictate predictable patterns for our sustained heritage and legacies.

OUR PILLARS

Access to our valuable, rare, inimitable and non-substitutable resources or assets for foundational growth and leadership development.

OUR PLACE

Our compelling and pristine environments that bring us to a place of selfawareness and personal convictions to stand up for a cause. These places activate our gifts and enhances the discovery of paths of purpose and leadership. They are our well-designed places for meditation, revitalization of conscience, and a place for reflections on life, family, country, life and life after the earth walk.

OUR PIONEERING-PRISM

Encouraging innovations, multi-dimensional perspectives, disruptive ideas that topple the predictions of experts and stretches the boundaries of social constructs and conventional thinking. We push against belief system, and intellectual boundaries that limit the human potential. We free the human will to discover hidden treasures and glory by challenging imposed cultural limitations. We advance the human race and bring progress to our communities.
