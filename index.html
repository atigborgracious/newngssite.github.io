THE 7Ps EXPERIENCE
AT DREAMSAFRICA

OUR PHILOSOPHY

Benefit from our rich curriculum which is designed with individual uniqueness and self concept in mind. Our curriculum provides wisdom & understanding for your self-worth, self-esteem, gifts, purpose and leadership.

OUR PEOPLE

Engage and interact with like-minded young leaders who will provoke you to enterprising leadership. Find great Mentors, Sponsors, Mentees, Destiny Agents, Accountability Partners, Parents and Guardians to help and guide you in your assignments.

OUR PROCESSES

Embrace our sustainable processes that will challenge you to unleash your potential. You will develop the needed resilience, character of mind and will, and the capacity for mastery in your chosen spheres of influence.

OUR PRINCIPLES

Be trained with enduring principles that guarantee leadership success and fulfillment. Our enduring principles will shape your belief system and mindset, and teach you predictable patterns for sustained legacy.

OUR PILLARS

Gain access to our valuable, rare, inimitable and non-substitutable resources for your foundational growth and leadership development.

OUR PLACE

Experience our pristine environments for your meditation, self awareness, revitalization of conscience, and family retreats. These places are well-designed to help activate your gifts and to enhance your journey of self discovery.

OUR PIONEERING-PRISM

Be challenged by the diversity in our talent hotbeds to harness an open belief system for innovations. Find your voice while embracing multi-dimensional perspectives that stretch the boundaries of social constructs and conventional thinking.
