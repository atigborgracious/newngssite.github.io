At CRE we understand how important the minutest of details are, so we handle each task with acute precision and dedication. Years of experience along with timely customer service has helped us build a reputation for quality ensuring customers receive the best possible results in the shortest time possible.

Team

From digital to offset, from content to print through fulfillment and every critical step in between, our dedicated team provides strategic solutions for your printing needs. Our team of designers, account managers, prepress and print specialists hail from book manufacturing and publishing backgrounds and ensure that every project is perfectly executed to consistently produce high quality work.

Quality Policy

Quality has been a major contributor to our excellence and success. Our processes are geared to deliver products that meet high quality standards . Periodical audits are conducted to check compliance of the standards. Our aim is to achieve customer satisfaction and meet client requirements through continuous improvements of the Quality Management System, right from Paper, Print Quality, Binding Strength, Logistics and Customer Support.

India Cost Advantage

We operate the youngest and most advanced book presses in India together with binding facilities for soft and hardcover books. Being exporters of paper, we procure huge quantity stocks because of which we have good relations with the paper mills and paper merchants in India. This helps us in offering very competitive prices to our clients thereby reducing the total project cost to a great extent.
