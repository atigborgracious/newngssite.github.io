Frederick Nuku Attah, the ever evolving multi-talented musician popularly known as Spontaneous keys (Sponkeys) is a world renowned musician, music consultant, an artiste, music director, composer and Producer with SPONMUSIK. And also belongs to a multi-talented gospel group EXIT where he plays the keyboard . Sponkeys is a member of the renowned international recognized Accra Symphony Orchestra Sponkeys has played for and shared stages with the likes of Nii Okai, Edwin Dadson, Kwesi Oteng, McVangelist, Mr. Re, Micah Stampley, JannyBlack, Sandra Huson, Jay Kenny Twist and lots of local and international musicians. Sponkeys is the manager of a number of renowned gospel and inspirational artiste in Ghana. He creates with great passion and precision. with great passion and precision.
