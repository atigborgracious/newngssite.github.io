DECLARATION OF LOAN

Description of the Service:
Documentation of the debtor's approval of a speciﬁc debt to others.
Service Requirements:
  • Attendance of the Headquarters (the debtor).
  • Bring an adult to know the age of majority Know the headquarters and headquarters
The required documents:
  • UAE ID card for people with ties and witnesses.
  • Submit a religion document.
Special Conditions:
  • The requet, clients and witnesses shall be presented to the judge for consideration and all parties shall be sworn in before the judge.
